What We Do / Core Capabilities
----------------------------------------------------------------
Hubble AI specializes in end-to-end AI and software product development. We design, build, deploy, and scale intelligent systems across different business domains and project sizes.

1. AI Automation & Intelligent Agents  
- Design and development of AI workflows, chatbots, voice agents, and agentic systems  
- Automation of repetitive tasks, internal operations, HR workflows, customer support, and decision-making pipelines  
- Solutions scale from small AI automations for startups to enterprise-grade autonomous systems  

2. AI Product Development  
- Full lifecycle AI product development: ideation, architecture, model design, development, testing, deployment, and scaling  
- Expertise in NLP, Computer Vision, and multimodal AI systems  
- Capable of building MVPs, production-ready AI platforms, and enterprise-level AI products  

3. Machine Learning Engineering (ML Engineering)  
- End-to-end ML model development: data preparation, feature engineering, model training, evaluation, and optimization  
- Classical ML, deep learning, and transformer-based systems  
- Model deployment, monitoring, versioning, and performance optimization  
- MLOps pipelines for reproducibility, scalability, and continuous improvement  
- Fine-tuning, retraining, and optimization of models for real-world production workloads  

4. Full-Stack Web & Software Development  
- Backend systems using NestJS, FastAPI, MongoDB, and microservices architectures  
- Frontend development with Next.js, real-time dashboards, and responsive interfaces  
- Seamless integration of AI models, ML pipelines, and intelligent agents into web and software products  

5. Real-Time Intelligent Systems  
- AI systems capable of processing data and interacting in real time  
- Examples include live AI interviewers, real-time analytics platforms, interactive automation tools, and voice-based systems  
- Focus on performance, reliability, and human-like interactions at scale  

6. AI & ML Consulting and Strategy  
- AI and ML feasibility analysis, system design, and technical roadmapping  
- Guidance on data strategy, model selection, and infrastructure decisions  
- Support from proof-of-concept and MVP stages through enterprise deployment
